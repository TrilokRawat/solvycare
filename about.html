About SOLVY

SOLVY is a trusted cleaning solutions brand offering high-performance products for both commercial and household cleaning needs.

We manufacture and supply a wide range of cleaning chemicals designed for hotels, hospitals, offices, as well as everyday home cleaning applications.

Our product portfolio includes floor cleaners, kitchen degreasers, glass cleaners, bathroom cleaners, tap cleaners, and hand hygiene solutions—formulated to support effective cleaning, freshness, and routine maintenance.

At SOLVY, we focus on consistent quality, safe usage, and reliable packaging options for bulk buyers, distributors, and retail customers across India.
